# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need prior experience with SQL?**
A: No prior experience is required. This course starts from the basics and builds to advanced topics.

**Q: What software do I need?**
A: MySQL Workbench (installation guides provided), a text editor, and access to course materials.

**Q: How do I get help if I’m stuck?**
A: See the Support page for contact info, office hours, and additional resources.

**Q: Are assignments graded?**
A: Yes, each assignment and quiz has a rubric and point value.

**Q: Can I work ahead?**
A: Yes! All modules are available for self-paced learning.
